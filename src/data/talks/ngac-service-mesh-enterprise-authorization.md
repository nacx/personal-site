In this session at the [4th Annual Multi-Cloud Conference](https://www.nist.gov/news-events/events/2023/05/4th-multi-cloud-conference-and-workshop)
conference held at [NIST](https://www.nist.gov/) co-presented with Zack Butcher, we
introduced the role of Next Generation Access Control (NGAC) and Service mesh for enterprise-wide Authorization.
We explained the fundamentals of NGAC and the main advantages it brings over traditional authorization systems like
RBAC or ABAC, and did a practical demo showing how _Policy as Code_ can be implemented for
enterprise-wide authorization at scale.
