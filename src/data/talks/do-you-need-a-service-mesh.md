In this talk I explored what a service mesh is and what they can do for your microservice web
backends. Are the claims of observability, resiliency, and WAF features real? Are they useful during development,
production, or both? Using pictures and demos, we'll find out! This session also briefly covered how a service mesh
works, giving us a mental model with which to explore and evaluate after the talk.
